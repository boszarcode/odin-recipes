Soup

Description

In this recipe we will be making a hearty tomato soup.

This is a great winter warmer dish for all to enjoy using fresh, easy to find ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Sugar (if needed)

Steps

  1. Boil the tomatoes, onion, garlic, and broth.
  2. Run the mixture in a blender and place into a large bowl.
  3. Make a roux.
  4. Add the tomato mixture and season to taste with salt and sugar.
